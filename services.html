Virtual Individual Therapy

My Approach

Therapy with me is flexible, collaborative, and tailored to you. I draw from several evidence-based approaches to meet your unique needs and goals. Together, we'll work to:

  • Clarify your values
  • Set meaningful therapy goals
  • Build coping tools
  • Navigate life's challenges
  • Explore and embrace your authentic self

My approach is grounded in being person-centered, LGBTQIA+ affirming, and neurodiversity-affirming. I also incorporate elements of Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT) and Interpersonal Psychotherapy (IPT) to support emotional growth, connection, and resilience.

Who I Work With

I offer virtual individual therapy for adults and teens who are:

  • Managing anxiety, depression, burnout, or stress
  • Struggling with relationships, family dynamics, or friendships
  • Going through life changes or transitions
  • Exploring their identity or seeking to better understand themselves
  • Looking to improve their mental and emotional wellbeing

Whether you're facing specific challenges or just feeling ready to grow, therapy can help you feel more grounded, confident, and aligned with who you are.

  • Saturdays and Sundays

Virtual Group Therapy

Looking for connection, support, or a space where you truly belong? Group therapy can be a powerful way to build community, gain insight, and grow alongside others with shared experiences. I offer a range of virtual groups designed for neurodivergent and LGBTQIA+ individuals, each created to meet specific needs in safe, affirming spaces.

Is Group the Right Fit for You?

Group therapy is a great fit if you're looking to:

  • Feel less alone in your experiences
  • Connect with people who "get it"
  • Share and receive support in a judgment-free space
  • Practice social skills and self-expression
  • Learn tools to cope with everyday stressors
  • Build confidence and self-understanding

If a group resonates with you or you're curious to learn more, reach out — I'd love to talk with you about how to join and what to expect!

Coaching Services

Clarity. Confidence. Forward Momentum.

Coaching with me is a supportive space to help you move forward — whether you're feeling overwhelmed, navigating a transition, or just ready to build systems that work for your brain and your life. Together, we'll clarify what matters most to you, identify what's getting in the way, and build strategies that align with how you operate best.

Coaching is NOT therapy.

While I am a licensed psychologist, coaching is a separate, non-clinical service and is not provided under my clinical license. Therapy often focuses on healing and mental health treatment. Coaching, on the other hand, is action-oriented — focused on the present and future to help you reach your goals, improve your systems, and build momentum.

If you're looking for a space to problem-solve and stay accountable — coaching might be exactly what you need.

What You Can Expect

  • Clear goals and meaningful action steps
  • Support for focus, motivation, and follow-through
  • Tools for organization and time management
  • A nonjudgmental space to explore values and direction
  • Encouragement and accountability from someone who gets how different minds work

Coaching Can Help With:

  • Life transitions and career pivots
  • Executive functioning challenges or decision paralysis
  • Creating personalized routines and systems
  • Strengthening confidence, clarity, and direction
  • Exploring building a life that feels more like you

Coaching is a powerful space for personal and professional development — but it is not a substitute for therapy, mental health treatment, or crisis support. If clinical care is needed in addition to coaching, I'll be happy to refer you to other trusted licensed providers.
