My Approach

Therapy with me is flexible, collaborative, and tailored to you. I draw from several evidence-based approaches to meet your unique needs and goals. Together, we'll work to:

  • Clarify your values
  • Set meaningful therapy goals
  • Build coping tools
  • Navigate life's challenges
  • Explore and embrace your authentic self

My approach is grounded in being person-centered, LGBTQIA+ affirming, and neurodiversity-affirming. I also incorporate elements of Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT) and Interpersonal Psychotherapy (IPT) to support emotional growth, connection, and resilience.

Who I Work With

I offer virtual individual therapy for adults and teens who are:

  • Managing anxiety, depression, burnout, or stress
  • Struggling with relationships, family dynamics, or friendships
  • Going through life changes or transitions
  • Exploring their identity or seeking to better understand themselves
  • Looking to improve their mental and emotional wellbeing

Whether you're facing specific challenges or just feeling ready to grow, therapy can help you feel more grounded, confident, and aligned with who you are.

Coaching

Clarity. Confidence. Forward Momentum.

Feeling stuck, overwhelmed, or ready for change? Coaching offers a supportive, action-focused space to help you move forward in a way that works for your brain.

Together, we’ll clarify what matters most, identify roadblocks, and build personalized strategies for focus, follow-through, and meaningful progress.

What to Expect

  • Clear goals and meaningful action steps
  • Support for focus, motivation, and follow-through
  • Tools for organization and time management
  • A nonjudgmental space to explore values and direction
  • Encouragement and accountability from someone who gets how different minds work

Great for:

  • Life transitions and career pivots
  • Decision paralysis & focus challenges
  • Building sustainable systems & habits
  • Aligning your life with your values

Note: Coaching is not therapy.

While I’m a licensed psychologist, coaching is a separate, non-clinical service. Therapy focuses on healing and mental health. Coaching is about taking action and creating momentum. If clinical care is needed, I can help refer you to other trusted licensed providers.

Consultation, Training & Workshops

Creating spaces where all minds and identities are valued.

I offer consultation, training, and workshops for clinicians, organizations, and communities committed to building more inclusive, neurodiversity-affirming, and culturally responsive environments.

  • For Therapists & Providers

    Clinical Consultation

    Support for mental health professionals and care teams looking to integrate neurodiversity-affirming practices into their work. Common focus areas include:

    • Creating inclusive, client-centered care models
    • Building sensory-friendly and accessible environments
    • Using affirming language and avoiding pathologizing approaches
    • Reviewing conceptualization and treatment plans through a neuro-affirming lens
    • Supporting clients with autism, ADHD, dyslexia, and more

  • For Organizations & Communities

    Workplace & Community Consultation

    Build a culture where neurodivergent and marginalized individuals can thrive. I partner with businesses, schools, and community groups to create environments that are inclusive, equitable, and effective.

    What I Offer:

    • Leadership & Staff Training:
      Equip your team with the tools to support and retain neurodivergent talent through identity-aware practices and psychologically safe environments.
    • Interactive Workshops:
      Customizable, hands-on sessions that foster understanding, collaboration, and lasting change. Topics include neurodiversity, executive functioning, sensory inclusion, and more.

Available workshop topics include:

  • Cultivating a Neuro-Affirming Culture
    An introduction to neurodiversity and how to design more inclusive spaces, teams, and systems that embrace cognitive and behavioral differences.
  • The Neurodiverse Love Languages
    Explore how different neurotypes express connection and care — and how to build more authentic relationships across communication styles.
  • Masking & Unmasking: Being Your Authentic Self
    Learn how masking impacts well-being and discover ways to foster environments where individuals can safely show up as their full selves.
  • Disability, Neurodiversity & What It Means to Lead
    Explore leadership through a lens of accessibility and equity — and how to create workplaces where disabled and neurodivergent individuals can succeed and belong.
  • Take Care Before You Don't Care
    A timely session on preventing burnout and compassion fatigue through sustainable, personalized self-care and community care strategies.
  • Forging Neuro-Affirming Communities on Campus
    Ideal for colleges and universities — learn how interdepartmental collaboration can support the growing population of neurodivergent students in higher ed.
